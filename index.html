This Website!

Web Design

When I set out to enter the world of Web Development. I knew I would need to establish my own space on the web. After several revisions and re-designs I decided to finally launch this site as an ever evolving work in progress.

It all began with a Graphista template. Props to them for providing us with lots of great resources. I saw some things that I liked and began chopping away until what was left is the site you're on now. One of the things I love about this craft is the ability to be creative and expressive while getting to do work I love. In this case, since it's my personal page, this allowed me to include photography from my father as opposed to having to find some third-party resource. Always great to be able to incorperate things close to me into my designs. I believe the web could use a bit more personality.

If you enjoy the photography featured here be sure to visit the flickr page of Art O'Connell...after all YouGottaHaveArt.

Mouse Rat

Web Design

This is a homepage for the former Indiana rock band Mouse Rat. The unofficial site of the one of my favorite fictional bands Parks and Recreation's Mouse Rat is always good for laughs and as odd as it is decent tunes

Made with Bootstrap 4 From a technical perspective it's simple and to the point, though where I believe this design really shines, is its clear concise focus on the content. The band.

I used Bootstrap to give it its responsive properties, much like many modern sites do. I tailored this project specifically towards a band for the purposes of this portfolio, though you'll quickly realise that this layout could be used for a variety fo small business or personal brand style web sites.

Veridian Dynamics

Web Design

This is another site template designed to showcase the work of clients who need a combination of clear information and creativity. Of course its extremly flexible in its application and could be customised to fit a wide variety of needs. My favorite aspects of this design are the opportunities to showcase several large media elements in an elegant yet impactful manner.

I find that nowadays, with the takeover of social media, individuals and businesses need to go the extra mile to effectively communicate with their target audience. Simply posting a picture, or listing some features doesn't cut it anymore.

This layout allows you to speak your mind and add insight to the media you share on your site. Not only can you showcase your beautiful, efficent product or service, you can build value around it by connecting with your audience.
